📊 HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** developed in **Microsoft Power BI** to help organizations analyze workforce data and identify employee attrition trends. The dashboard provides actionable insights into employee demographics, department-wise attrition, job satisfaction, and workforce distribution through dynamic and interactive visualizations.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate how HR data can be transformed into meaningful business insights that support strategic decision-making and employee retention initiatives.

---

🎯 Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition across different departments and job roles.
* Monitor key HR metrics through interactive KPI cards.
* Explore workforce demographics by age group and gender.
* Evaluate employee satisfaction across various job roles.
* Enable dynamic filtering for deeper HR analysis.

📊 Key Business Insights

* Sales department has the highest employee attrition.
* Employees aged **25–34** represent the largest workforce segment.
* Sales Representatives have the highest attrition rate.
* Employees with a Life Sciences education background contribute the highest attrition count.
* Job satisfaction varies significantly across different job roles.
